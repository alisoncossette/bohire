# BoHire — Go-To-Market Strategy

> "Get the supply right, and the demand follows."

---

## The Core Insight

Two-sided marketplace dynamics: **Recruiters won't pay unless candidates are there. Candidates won't show up unless recruiters are there.**

Solution: Solve the supply side first. Make verified candidate profiles genuinely valuable *to the candidate* — their profile is their portfolio, not just a recruiter feed. Then sell scarcity: "1,200 verified SF engineers. Request access."

---

## Phase 1: SF Candidate Supply (Month 1–3)

**Goal:** 5,000 verified candidate profiles in SF tech

### Target: SF Tech Candidates
- Software engineers, PMs, designers (technical)
- Recent bootcamp grads, CS seniors at Berkeley/Stanford/SFSU
- Senior engineers open to opportunities but not actively job hunting

### Channels

| Channel                        | Tactic                                                          | Est. Cost | Target Volume |
|--------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|-----------|---------------|
| **Product Hunt launch**        | Day-1 launch. "Replace your resume with your actual code."     | $0        | 500–2K signups|
| **Hacker News (Show HN)**      | Show HN: "I built a GitHub-native resume replacement"           | $0        | 500–1K        |
| **Tech Slack/Discord communities** | Locally Optimistic, SF Tech, Rands Leadership, Dev circles | $0        | 300–800       |
| **Twitter/X seeding**          | Founder-led content. Ship in public. Tag WorldID, Bolospot.    | $0        | 500–2K        |
| **Coding bootcamps**           | Partner with App Academy, Hack Reactor, Flatiron (SF cohorts)  | $500/mo   | 100–300/mo    |
| **University CS programs**     | UC Berkeley, Stanford CS, SFSU — career fair presence          | $2K/semester | 200–500/event |
| **Referral program**           | "Invite 3 devs → get verified faster + early access badge"     | ~$5/referral | Viral loop   |

### Candidate Acquisition Cost
- Organic/community: **~$0–$5 per profile**
- Paid (bootcamp partnerships): **~$15–$25 per profile**
- Blended CAC for candidate: **~$5–$10** (target)
- 5K profiles @ $7 blended = **~$35K total spend**

### What Makes Candidates Show Up
1. Profile is *their* asset — shareable link, portfolio-grade
2. Verified badge is a signal of quality, not just another platform
3. They control access — recruiters can't spam them
4. No resume PDF required ever again

## Phase 2: Embed with 5 Companies (Month 2–4)

**Goal:** 5 companies actively using BoHire to hire. One already has a lighthouse prototyping.

### Target Company Profile
- 20–500 employees
- Tech-forward (use Notion, Linear, Vercel, etc.)
- Active hiring: 3–20 open roles
- Frustrated with current tools (Greenhouse + LinkedIn = expensive + noisy)

### How to Land Them
- **Lighthouse customer first:** Already prototyping. Get to signed pilot → get case study rights
- **Founder direct outreach:** CEOs and Heads of Eng at SF startups. Warm intros via angels
- **VC portfolio angle:** Introduce to investors early, ask for 3 portfolio co intros each
- **Recruiter-led:** Individual recruiters self-serve on Starter plan → evangelize internally

### Free Pilot Structure
- 60-day pilot: unlimited bolo requests, white-glove onboarding
- Success metric: Did they make at least one hire from BoHire?
- Exit: Convert to Growth or Enterprise plan, or get a case study + referral

### What We Learn in Phase 2
- Which roles convert best (IC engineer, not exec)
- Friction in the bolo request flow
- What AI assessment signals recruiters actually use
- Candidate dropout rate in permission flow

## Recruiter Sales Motion

### Bottom-Up (Self-Serve, Primary)
- Recruiter finds BoHire via a candidate's shared profile link
- Signs up for Starter ($299/mo) — no sales call needed
- Tries bolo request → gets verified candidate profile → impressed
- Upsells to Growth when team expands

**Conversion funnel:**
- Profile view → free trial signup: 5%
- Free trial → Starter paid: 25%
- Starter → Growth (3 months): 15%
- Growth → Enterprise (12 months): 8%

### Top-Down (Sales-Assisted, for Growth/Enterprise)
- Outbound to Heads of Talent at 50–200 person companies
- Sequence: LinkedIn DM → email → "I'll show you 10 verified candidates matching your open role"
- Demo is the product: show live candidate profiles + bolo flow
- Pitch: "You're paying $15K/seat for LinkedIn Recruiter. This is $800/mo and the candidates are verified."

**Enterprise path:**
- Recruiting agencies (own the recruiter relationship, not just one company)
- Direct enterprise: 500+ employee companies with dedicated talent teams
- Custom pricing: $3K–$15K/mo depending on seat count + request volume

## What We're NOT Doing (Yet)

- **No paid ads at launch** — community + product-led first
- **No enterprise sales before product-market fit** — pilot-first
- **No international expansion in Y1** — SF → NYC before London
- **No exec-level placements** — IC roles only in Y1 (faster cycles, better signal)

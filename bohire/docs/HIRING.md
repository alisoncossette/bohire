## Founding Team — Current Gaps

Before making any hires, the founders need to cover:
- Product strategy + fundraising (CEO/founder)
- Core technical architecture: Bolospot integration, GitHub analysis, World ID flow (CTO/founder)

**Known gaps from Day 1:**
- AI/ML depth for code assessment quality
- Growth / candidate acquisition (not a dev skill)
- Recruiter-side sales (needs someone who's been a recruiter or sold to them)

If the founding team doesn't include a strong ML/AI technical co-founder, Hire #1 is non-negotiable.

## Compensation Philosophy

### Pre-Seed / Seed (Y1)
- **Cash:** Below market (60–75% of market rate), founders included
- **Equity:** Aggressive early grants. First 5 hires should feel like co-founders.
- **Vesting:** 4 years, 1-year cliff. Standard.
- **Upside narrative:** "If this works, your 0.5% is worth $5M at a $1B exit."

### Post-Seed (Y2+)
- **Cash:** At or approaching market rate as funding lands
- **Equity:** Still meaningful but smaller grants (0.1–0.3% for senior individual contributors)
- **Benefits:** Remote-flexible, health insurance, learning budget ($2K/yr), home office stipend

### Guiding Principles
1. Pay enough that money isn't a distraction
2. Equity should feel real, not like lottery tickets
3. Hire people who'd take a pay cut to work on this — then don't make them regret it
4. No rockstar hiring — hire people who make everyone around them better

## Key Role Deep Dives

### AI/ML Engineer — What "Good" Looks Like
This person needs to solve: *How do you assess engineering ability from a GitHub profile?*
- Not just "how many commits" — that's noise
- Real signals: code review quality, PR description quality, test coverage, architecture decisions, language breadth vs. depth
- Should have experience with: code embeddings, LLM evals, semantic analysis of code
- Red flag: "I'll just call the OpenAI API" as the entire plan

### Growth Lead — What "Good" Looks Like
Developer trust is hard-won and easily lost. This person needs:
- Genuine credibility in dev communities (not marketing to devs — talking WITH devs)
- Experience with community-led growth (Discord, Slack, newsletters)
- Has personally done a successful Product Hunt launch
- Will not spam. Will not fake engagement. Will protect the brand.

### Recruiter Sales — What "Good" Looks Like
Recruiters are deeply skeptical of new tools. They've been burned by ATS promises.
- Must be able to demo live in 15 minutes and let the product speak
- Best hire: former recruiter who was frustrated with LinkedIn Recruiter costs
- Should understand both sides: why candidates hate applying and why recruiters hate reviewing 500 resumes
- Commission structure tied to MRR growth, not just new logos

## What NOT to Hire For (Yet)

- **Legal/compliance full-time:** Use Clerky, Stripe Atlas, and an outside counsel on retainer
- **Finance/CFO:** Hire a fractional CFO after $1M ARR
- **Marketing manager:** Growth lead handles this in Y1
- **DevRel headcount beyond the Growth Lead:** The founders should be doing DevRel personally until PMF is clear
- **HR/People Ops:** Tackle this when you hit 15+ people
